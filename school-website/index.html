Welcome To Moorside High School

Believe, Achieve, Succeed

Together

As the Head Master it gives me great pleasure to welcome you to the website of what I believe to be a fantastic school. Moorside High School is located on the state of the art Moorside Campus and as a school; we embrace the latest technologies, facilities and equipment to provide a platform for our students to flourish.

As a leader with a clear moral purpose and a strong educational philosophy, I believe it is our job at Moorside High School to ensure that our students leave us ready to not only contribute, but to shine in what is a very competitive and challenging world around us. In order to do so, we need to have a broad educational offer from Year 7 to 11, complimented by a rich array of extra-curricular opportunities to further instil a love of learning. At the heart of my philosophy is a commitment to working in the very best interests of our students and ensuring that everybody at Moorside High School serves our community to the best of our ability.

Finally, I would like to reiterate my sense of pride in being appointed to the position of Head Master. I want to state very clearly that my ambition is for Moorside High School to become a leading education establishment, not just in the local area but much further afield too.


Mr. C Davis
Principal, Moorside High School
BA Hons - NPHQ
